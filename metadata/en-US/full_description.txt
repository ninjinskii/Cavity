<i>Cavity</i> is a wine cellar manager for Android.

A stock management app for wine cellars, that lets you always pick the right bottle at the right time.

<b>Features:</b>

* Stock management
* Highlights peaking bottles
* Stock history
* Handle friends (Give a bottle to someone, share with a friend...)
* Buy & consumptions stats
* Search
* Tasting sessions: decanting notification, chill a bottle
* Auto online backups (optionnal: requires an account)
